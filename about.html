About

Allison G. Stroud

Founder, Redsong Consulting
AI · Automation · Analytics

I'm a business intelligence and analytics leader with 10+ years building and scaling high-performing analytics teams and 20+ years of broader technology leadership across Operations, Supply Chain, and Finance.

My work is rooted in one belief: the right tools, connected to the right workflows, should free people to do the work that actually matters. I've spent my career translating complex business problems into practical systems — and I founded Redsong Consulting to bring that same caliber of thinking to businesses that are ready to stop operating on manual.

I built analytics and automation functions from the ground up at Goldman Sachs, MillerKnoll, and Perrigo. Now I do the same for growing businesses across West Michigan and beyond — on a scale that fits.
